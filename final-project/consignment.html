Consignment Introduced May 2020

What is Consignment?

Consigning with us would mean offering your product to be sold on our website for a minimal fee. If you had a hard time selling your products for any reason you can come to us and we will post it on our website. If your product sell through our website, we keep 5% of the sale and the you keep 95%. The consignment process is simple and simbiotic, as both parties expierence positivity. To begin the process, the buyer selects the price on his or her item and will then proceed to send us detailed pictures of the product. Then we will update the pictures onto our webpage and we take over from there with advertisement. We will alert you when the item is purchased.


What can you Consign?

As of now, you can only consign items that fall under the current categories in Aadi's Shop. If you have three or more products under a new category, contact us and we will gladly create a new category. Current categories include: Keyboards, Xbox.


Why should you Consign on Aadi's Shop?

Many other sites offer consignment, but our process is more efficient and safe. To begin, most places do not offer a 95 to 5 percent consigner to owner ratio. Ratios are typically 85 to 15 or 90 to 10. If you are interested, fill out the form at the bottom, and we will get back to you shortly.

Frequently Asked Questions:


How many items can I consign?

You can consign as many items as you would like!

I am unsure how much my item is worth. What do I do?

We will gladly assist you in assessing how much your product is worth using past data and products we have sold in the past.

What if my item is sold before it is sold on Aadi's Shop?

Please inform us if your item is sold. We will gladly remove your product from our site and hope that you continue to consign with us!
